# 🌍 Tripziii – Travel & Backpacking Website

Tripziii is a **travel and backpacking discovery website** designed to help users explore amazing destinations around **Bangalore and South India**.  
The platform allows travelers to discover destinations, explore travel packages, view galleries, and connect with a community of adventure seekers.

This project demonstrates a **multi-page responsive website** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, structured with organized folders and reusable assets.

---

# ✨ About Tripziii

At **Tripziii**, we believe every journey begins with a single step—and we’re here to make sure that step leads to unforgettable adventures.

Whether you're a seasoned traveler or just starting your backpacking journey, Tripziii helps you:

- Discover amazing destinations
- Explore travel packages
- Plan adventures
- Connect with a community of travelers

Bangalore is more than just a city—it is a **gateway to breathtaking destinations in South India**, including:

- Misty hill stations  
- Scenic waterfalls  
- Historical landmarks  
- Cultural hotspots  

Tripziii curates the **best backpacking experiences** to make travel easier and more exciting.

# ⚙ Workflow of the Project

The website follows a **multi-page navigation structure**.

### 1️⃣ Homepage (`index.html`)
Entry point of the website and highlights destinations and services.

### 2️⃣ About Page
Explains the Tripziii mission and travel philosophy.

### 3️⃣ Destination Page
Shows travel destinations around Bangalore.

### 4️⃣ Packages Page
Lists available travel packages.

### 5️⃣ Services Page
Displays travel services offered by Tripziii.

### 6️⃣ Gallery Page
Displays images from previous trips and destinations.

### 7️⃣ Booking Page
Allows users to plan and book trips.

### 8️⃣ Contact Page
Allows users to reach Tripziii for support or inquiries.

### 9️⃣ Team Page
Displays the team behind the platform.

### 🔟 Error Handling
Custom `404.html` page for broken links.
