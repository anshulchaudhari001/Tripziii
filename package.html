WEEKEND GETAWAY

Gokarna

A Tranquil Beach Escape When you think of a weekend on the beach, Goa might be the first place that comes to mind. But if you’re looking for something less commercialized and more peaceful, Gokarna is the perfect alternative. Just about 500 km from Bangalore, this quaint temple town is a haven for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty. With pristine beaches, stunning sunsets, and a laid-back vibe, Gokarna offers the perfect setting for a weekend of relaxation. Whether you want to unwind in a cozy beach shack, explore the local temples, or enjoy a quiet walk along the shoreline, Gokarna will not disappoint.

Hampi

Where History Meets Hippie Culture Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a must-visit for history buffs and cultural enthusiasts alike. Once the glorious capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi now stands as a captivating blend of ancient ruins and modern-day charm. Explore the intricately carved temples, marvel at the architectural brilliance, and lose yourself in the stories of the past. But Hampi isn’t just about history; cross the Tungabhadra River, and you’ll find yourself in a vibrant hippie town, where you can enjoy lively cafes, artistic performances, and a unique bohemian atmosphere.

Coorg

The Scotland of India Nestled amidst the lush green hills of the Western Ghats, Coorg is a paradise for nature lovers. Located just 270 km from Bangalore, this misty hill station is known for its coffee plantations, scenic waterfalls, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you’re trekking through dense forests, exploring the local Kodava culture, or simply enjoying the serene views, Coorg offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Goa

More Than Just Beaches Goa needs no introduction, but there’s so much more to this coastal state than its famous beaches. From its rich Portuguese heritage to its vibrant nightlife, Goa offers something for everyone. Explore the baroque architecture, indulge in the local cuisine, or simply relax on the sandy shores with a cocktail in hand. Whether you’re an adventurer, a history buff, or a party-goer, Goa will cater to all your whims.

Chikmagalur

Coffee Country Chikmagalur, known as the ‘Coffee Land of Karnataka,’ is a serene hill station set against the backdrop of the majestic Mullayangiri ranges. With its sprawling coffee plantations, mist-covered hills, and cascading waterfalls, Chikmagalur offers a peaceful retreat in the lap of nature. Whether you’re hiking through the hills, visiting ancient temples, or simply soaking in the scenic beauty, Chikmagalur provides a refreshing break from the city’s chaos.

Wayanad

The Lush Green Haven Just across the border in Kerala, Wayanad is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of nature and adventure. Known as the ‘Spice Garden of India,’ Wayanad is dotted with tea estates, wildlife sanctuaries, and picturesque hills. Whether you’re exploring the prehistoric Edakkal Caves, trekking to the Banasura Sagar Dam, or camping under the stars, Wayanad promises a weekend full of discovery and excitement.

Kodaikanal

The Princess of Hill Stations Kodaikanal, often referred to as the ‘Princess of Hill Stations,’ is one of the most popular destinations in South India. With its cool climate, stunning landscapes, and laid-back vibe, Kodaikanal is the perfect place for a romantic retreat or a solo escape. Explore the shimmering lakes, stroll through lush forests, and take in the breathtaking views that this charming town has to offer.

LONG-WEEKEND GETAWAY

Gokarna

Serenity on the Sands When you think of a weekend by the beach, Goa might be the first place that pops into your mind. But if you’re seeking an alternative to the bustling vibe of Goa, Gokarna is your perfect escape. This sleepy temple town, located about 500 km from Bangalore, offers a serene retreat from the demands of daily life. Known for its unspoiled beaches, Gokarna is a haven for those looking to unwind and reconnect with nature. Whether it’s the allure of the Mahabaleshwara Temple or the promise of unadulterated beaches overlooking the Arabian Sea, Gokarna has something for everyone. You can embark on an adventurous trek along the coastline, savor global cuisines at beachside cafes, or simply lose yourself in the laid-back hippie culture that permeates the town. And when the sun sets, whether you’re dancing under the stars or strolling beside the glowing phytoplanktons, Gokarna offers moments of pure bliss.

Dandeli

The Adventure Seeker’s Dream Nestled on the banks of the Kali River in the Uttar Kannada district of Karnataka, Dandeli is a hidden gem for thrill-seekers. This quaint town, surrounded by dense forests, is rich in biodiversity and offers the perfect backdrop for a long weekend filled with adventure. Dandeli is steeped in legend, with its name derived from the tale of Dandeleppa, a devoted servant who fell in love with a lady from the Mirashi landlord family. The tragic love story adds a mystical aura to the place, but Dandeli’s true appeal lies in its natural beauty and the plethora of activities it offers. From exploring the unexplored trails of the Western Ghats to indulging in exhilarating water sports, Dandeli promises an unforgettable experience for those craving excitement and connection with nature. Whether you're in the mood for a tranquil beach retreat or an adventurous getaway, these long weekend destinations from Bangalore provide the perfect escape. So, pack your bags, leave your worries behind, and set off on a journey that will refresh your soul and ignite your spirit of adventure.
