Gokarna: A Tranquil Beach Escape

When you think of a weekend on the beach, Goa might be the first place that comes to mind. But if you’re looking for something less commercialized and more peaceful, Gokarna is the perfect alternative. Just about 500 km from Bangalore, this quaint temple town is a haven for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty. With pristine beaches, stunning sunsets, and a laid-back vibe, Gokarna offers the perfect setting for a weekend of relaxation. Whether you want to unwind in a cozy beach shack, explore the local temples, or enjoy a quiet walk along the shoreline, Gokarna will not disappoint.

Hampi: Where History Meets Hippie Culture

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a must-visit for history buffs and cultural enthusiasts alike. Once the glorious capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi now stands as a captivating blend of ancient ruins and modern-day charm. Explore the intricately carved temples, marvel at the architectural brilliance, and lose yourself in the stories of the past. But Hampi isn’t just about history; cross the Tungabhadra River, and you’ll find yourself in a vibrant hippie town, where you can enjoy lively cafes, artistic performances, and a unique bohemian atmosphere.

Coorg: The Scotland of India

Nestled amidst the lush green hills of the Western Ghats, Coorg is a paradise for nature lovers. Located just 270 km from Bangalore, this misty hill station is known for its coffee plantations, scenic waterfalls, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you’re trekking through dense forests, exploring the local Kodava culture, or simply enjoying the serene views, Coorg offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
